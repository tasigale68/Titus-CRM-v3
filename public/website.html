FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Titus CRM?
Titus CRM is an AI-powered all-in-one platform built specifically for NDIS, aged care and community service providers in Australia. It combines rostering, client management, billing, compliance tracking, recruitment, HR, and learning management into a single platform.
How much does Titus CRM cost?
Titus CRM starts from $69/week for sole traders and small providers, with plans up to $799/week for large registered providers. All plans include a 14-day free trial. There is a one-off $1,999 + GST onboarding fee.
Does Titus CRM replace my current rostering software?
Yes. Titus includes a full rostering and scheduling module with SCHADS Award compliance, shift management, and AI-assisted scheduling — replacing standalone rostering platforms.
Is Titus CRM NDIS compliant?
Yes. Titus is built from the ground up for NDIS compliance, including SCHADS Award calculations, incident reporting, progress notes, audit logs, and participant data management.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored on Australian servers in the Sydney region. Titus meets Australian data sovereignty requirements for NDIS participant information.
Can Titus handle both employees and contractors?
Yes. Titus manages TFN employees and independent contractors from one scheduler with separate payment tracks — SCHADS-compliant payroll for employees and auto-generated invoices for contractors.
What AI features does Titus have?
AI is built into the core of Titus — including AI report writing, CV scanning, document scanning, voice agents, call transcript analysis, and SMS triage.
How long does onboarding take?
Most providers are fully live within one week. We handle data migration and provide a single training session for your team.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to every feature.
What makes Titus different from other NDIS software?
Titus is the only NDIS platform with AI built into every module, flat weekly pricing that matches how NDIS funding works, and a genuine all-in-one design — not bolt-on integrations.
